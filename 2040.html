Superskin

基因工程的變革＿換膚技術

到了 2040 年，
人類已不滿足於陸地生活
為了創造出更加多元的生活體驗，開闢出由空中樂園、陸生叢林、水底王宮組成的三大世界，並且可以自由穿梭在不同的世界之中。 要適應不同的世界，基因工程學家們研發出一種暫時性的仿生皮膚，模仿鳥類的翅膀以適應空中飛行、模擬魚類在水中的游動方式，並命名為換膚技術 SuperSkin。針對如此的創舉，開發者 CELL 只留下一句話：「 我相信物種間的共融是未來生活的解方。」以及發表劃時代的新產品「 智能衣櫥 Celluit 」。 智能衣櫥是執行 SuperSkin 的載體，除了可以穿戴與模擬不同的皮膚特性，更搭載模擬駕訓系統，人們需通過相對應的 SKIN TEST 才可以擁有皮膚特性，以熟悉非原生的骨骼肌肉操作。 為了提供人們能自由地切換皮膚狀態，智能衣櫥廣泛遍布於路邊，也因此利用 AI 辨識系統來確認身分。 基因工程與智能科技是未來社會的重點技術，人類透過學習其他物種的生存方式以獲得更大的生存空間與機會，「 換膚技術 SuperSkin 」即是劃時代的解答，打造出屬於全人類的 WeWe Future。

In 2040, human beings are no longer satisfied with living on land. In order to create a diverse life experience, human has opened up three major worlds consisting of a paradise in the air, a terrestrial jungle, and an underwater palace, and you can freely shuttle between different worlds. To adapt to different worlds, genetic engineers have developed a temporary bionic skin, imitating the wings of birds to adapt to flying in the air, simulating the way fish swim in water, and named it SuperSkin. In response to such a pioneering work, the developer CELL left only said: "I believe that the integration of species is the solution to future life." And released an epoch-making new product "smart wardrobe Celluit". The smart wardrobe is the carrier for implementing SuperSkin. In addition to wearing and simulating different skin characteristics, it is also equipped with a simulated driving training system. People need to pass the corresponding SKIN TEST to familiarize themselves with non-native skeletal muscle operations, and to have skin characteristics. In order to provide people with the freedom to switch skin status, smart wardrobes are widely spread on the road, and uses AI identification systems to confirm identities. Genetic engineering and intelligent technology are the key technologies of the future society. Human beings gain greater living space and opportunities by learning the survival methods of other species. "SuperSkin skin-changing technology" is an epoch-making answer, creating a WeWe Future that belongs to all mankind. .
